Virtual Tour

VISIBEL.AI

Welcome to the Visibel AI Virtual Tour. Explore a space where artificial intelligence is made transparent, ethical, and easy to understand. From the innovation room to the monitoring center, each area reflects our mission: to develop AI systems that are not only smart but also visible and trustworthy. Take a look around — and discover how Visibel AI brings clarity to technology.

VISIBEL.AI BUILDING

The Visibel.ai Building is a digital innovation hub that showcases how AI can be transparent and ethical.

MAIN LOBBY

The Main Lobby of Visibel.ai serves as the welcoming gateway to a transparent AI ecosystem. Designed with openness and clarity in mind, this space introduces visitors to the core principles of Visibel AI: ethical innovation, explainability, and human-centered technology.

MONITORING ROOM

The Monitoring Room is the control center where the performance and behavior of AI systems are observed in real time. Equipped with advanced dashboards and analytics tools, this room ensures that all AI operations are transparent, ethical, and aligned with user expectations.

MEETING ROOM

The Meeting Room at Visibel AI is a collaborative space designed for brainstorming, strategy sessions, and team discussions. It fosters open communication and creativity, enabling developers, researchers, and stakeholders to align on ethical AI development and innovative solutions.

DATA CENTER

The Data Center at Visibel.ai is a secure and high-performance facility where all data powering the AI systems is stored and managed. It ensures data integrity, privacy, and availability, supporting real-time processing and analysis for transparent AI operations.

CONTAINMENT

A Containment Server is a specialized system designed to isolate, monitor, and control potentially risky processes or applications, especially in AI or cybersecurity environments. Its primary role is to “contain” any harmful or unexpected behavior to prevent it from affecting other systems or data..
